WELCOME TO FIRST FIDDLE



First Fiddle Restaurants, one of the world’s largest F&B companies in the casual dining sector, is headed by Priyank Sukhija. From Lazeez Affair, the first brand conceived in 1999 to Lord Of The Drinks, Dragonfly Experience, and Plum By Bent Chair, our latest ventures, we have brought a wave of innovation in the hospitality industry throughout. Being the world’s largest restaurant chain with over 30+ restaurants, now we are open to franchising routes for expansion and soon hitting the international grounds too.
